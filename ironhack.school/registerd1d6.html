Welcome to Ironhack Student Platform

Registering with Ironhack Student Platform gives you access to all of our current and future free courses. Not ready to take a course just yet? Registering puts you on our mailing list - we will update you as courses are added.

Next Steps

As part of joining Ironhack Student Platform, you will receive an email message with instructions for activating your account. Don't see the email? Check your spam folder and mark Ironhack Student Platform emails as 'not spam'. At Ironhack Student Platform, we communicate mostly through email.
